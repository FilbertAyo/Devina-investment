About us

Our Story

Devina Investment Group is a diversified Tanzanian-based company dedicated to delivering value across multiple sectors including Agriculture, Electronics, and Finance. Since our founding in 2023, we have focused on building sustainable solutions that empower communities, drive innovation, and support economic growth.

In the agricultural sector, we specialize in the distribution, sales, and supply of high-quality agricultural products and pesticides, working closely with farmers to enhance productivity and market access. Our electronics division provides a wide range of small home appliances and modern devices tailored to improve everyday living. In finance, we offer accessible and reliable services designed to help individuals and businesses manage, grow, and invest their resources effectively.

  • Reliable supply and distribution of agricultural products and pest control solutions
  • Modern electronics and small appliances for home and business use
  • Accessible financial services that support personal and business growth

At Devina Investment Group, our mission is to provide dependable solutions across industries, foster local partnerships, and contribute to the long-term prosperity of the communities we serve. We are committed to innovation, quality, and excellence in everything we do.
