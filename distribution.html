Distribution Services

At Devina Investment Group, we ensure the timely and efficient distribution of agricultural inputs and produce across rural and urban markets. Our distribution network is built to support farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses by delivering quality products directly to their location.

From seeds, fertilizers, and agrochemicals to fresh produce and processed goods, we manage end-to-end logistics with transparency and professionalism. Our goal is to reduce post-harvest losses, improve supply chain efficiency, and bridge the gap between producers and markets.

“ASU Devina helped us reach markets we never thought possible. Their distribution support is a game changer for smallholder farmers like us.”

Juma Mwakalinga

Local Farmer – Mbeya, Tanzania

With our fleet of delivery vehicles and experienced logistics team, we serve both local and cross-regional distribution needs. Our partners can rely on consistent supply, competitive pricing, and on-time delivery.

We are committed to empowering agriculture through modern distribution solutions that support food security and sustainable farming practices.
